Dilki Sewwandi

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC), Sri Lanka. My work focuses on making online spaces safer, particularly in the context of deepfakes and their impact on communities in the Global South. Currently I explore human-centered deepfake literacy interventions under the guidance of my advisor, Dr Dilrukshi Gamage.

I have a publication at CHI, which marked my first research collaboration as a co-author. Since then, I have attended multiple international conferences and summer schools, expanding my research network and learning from like-minded researchers across the world.

I'm passionate about HCI, digital safety, and designing technologies that empower people and support safer digital spaces.
